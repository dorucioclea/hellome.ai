Join our community of ambassadors and complete missions to earn free books and exciting rewards. Can’t wait to get involved? Read on…

### What is a HelloMe.ai Ambassador?

A HelloMe.ai Ambassador is anyone who loves reading with a little bookworm in their life and wants to share their storytime passion with others! Ambassadors complete regular missions from creating fun videos to spreading the word about HelloMe.ai. The more missions you complete, the more rewards you receive!

### Who can be a HelloMe.ai Ambassador?

Anyone! You might be a parent, grandparent or even a super-aunt or uncle. You can be any age, live anywhere in the world and things like social media followers really aren’t important. All that matters is that you love books and HelloMe.ai.

### What are the benefits?

If you join our amazing community of ambassadors you’ll get to be part of our HelloMe.ai world – helping to share the magic of reading with people everywhere. You’ll also have the chance to earn free books and HelloMe.ai book tokens to spend on future purchases.
